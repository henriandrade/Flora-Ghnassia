About

Flora Ghnassia is a passionate UX/UI & Product Designer crafting intuitive, scalable digital experiences since 2016.

Flora’s experience spans both in-house roles and running her own design studio, giving her a unique blend of collaborative product development and creative leadership.
With proficiency in both design & work management tools, she approaches problem-solving through a multidisciplinary lens, seamlessly integrating her background in illustration and motion design to craft memorable, playful & engaging experiences that meet both user needs and business goals.
She thrives in cross-functional environments, working closely with agency clients, engineers, product managers, and researchers to deliver data-driven, iterative, quantifiable design solutions.
A tech enthusiast, Flora is also fascinated by the potential of AI technologies, and opportunities that push creative and technical boundaries.
